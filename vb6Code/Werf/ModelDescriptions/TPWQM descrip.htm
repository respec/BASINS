General Description

TPWQM is a one-dimensional tidal average model with branching capability. The model is suitable for narrow, elongated tidal creeks or basins.

Model methods include the following:

The physical transport process is effected by tidal flushing, which requires solution of algebraic equations. The solution of equations for biochemical processes are obtained by analytical solutions. The computations of the physical and biochemical processes are decoupled (Park et al. 1998)

Limitations

Limited to sectionally homogeneous tidal creeks with length much less than tidal wave length, e.g., less than 25 km for a 3 m deep creek.

Application History

Applied extensively to Virginia small coastal basins.

Model Use Features

The only hydrodynamic input is tidal range. Has Windows-based interface for Virginia small coastal basins.

Model Support

Users Manual available, little additional support.
